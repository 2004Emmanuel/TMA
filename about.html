About Us

The MindsetNg is a youth led innovative social enterprise with the motive of supporting children and youths in disadvantaged communities with leadership skills and resources neccessary for them to become proactice agent of change in their local communities focusing on global citizenship education. we are based in Lagos Nigeria.
